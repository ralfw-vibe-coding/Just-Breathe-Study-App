title: "5 Pillars of Well-being"
overview: "Five areas of life that quiet practices are meant to support."
---

Quiet practices are presented in the manual as support for five critical pillars of well-being:
- Physical
- Mental & Emotional
- Social
- Spiritual
- Environmental

The model broadens the purpose of practice beyond stress relief alone. Breathwork, meditation, deep rest, and shared practice are meant to support the wider conditions that shape human well-being.

Important to remember:
- Well-being is multi-dimensional.
- Practice is strongest when it supports daily life as a whole, not only isolated moments of calm.
