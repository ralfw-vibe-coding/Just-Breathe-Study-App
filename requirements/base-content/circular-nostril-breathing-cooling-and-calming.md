**How to practice**
1. Sit comfortably and lift the right hand to the nose.
2. Close the right nostril with the thumb and inhale through the left nostril.
3. Close the left nostril with the ring finger, release the right nostril, and exhale through the right.
4. Continue in this way, always inhaling through the left and exhaling through the right, for 1-3 minutes.

**Purpose**
- create a cooling effect in the body
- calm the mind
- soften activation without collapsing alertness
- give attention a simple, repeatable breath loop

**Why it works**
- the one-direction nostril pattern creates a very clear mental and respiratory rhythm
- the slower, more attentive pace reduces scattered breathing
- the cooling emphasis helps reduce inner heat, agitation, and urgency
