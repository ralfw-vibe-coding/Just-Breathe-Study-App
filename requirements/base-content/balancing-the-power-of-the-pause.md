This ABC123 sequence uses pause-based techniques to create balance, pacing, and nervous-system steadiness.

Structure:
- begin with the Just Breathe invitation, stillness, awareness, observation of the breath, and a conscious breath
- `1. Box Breath` with count variations: `4:4:4:4`, `6:6:6:6`, `8:8:8:8`
- `2. Inspired Pause`
- `3. Alternate Nostril Breath`
- close with conscious breath, renewed awareness, stillness, and `3 Gratitudes`

Purpose:
- develop steadiness through counted rhythm
- make the pause feel more familiar and less reactive
- balance the system without pushing strongly up or down

Why the sequence works:
- box breathing establishes clear structure and equal pacing
- inspired pause makes the retention phase more directly felt
- alternate nostril breathing carries the balancing effect into a more flowing pattern
