The signature Just Breathe practice for down-regulation and nervous-system rest.

Structure:
- begin with the opening invitation, stillness, awareness, observation of the breath, and a conscious breath or physiological sigh
- move into the `XLT` breathwork protocol using `Extended Exhale`, `Humming Breath`, and `Triangle Breathing 4-7-8`
- continue into `Just Breathe Meditation`
- allow the practice to land in deep rest and stillness
- close with a conscious breath, renewed awareness, stillness, and `3 Gratitudes`

Timing:
- 10-minute version: 5 minutes breathwork, 5 minutes meditation
- 20-minute version: 10 minutes breathwork, 10 minutes meditation
- 45-minute version: 20 minutes breathwork, 20 minutes meditation, with extra time in stillness as needed

Purpose:
- manage stress and hyper-stimulation
- balance oxygen and carbon dioxide distribution
- shift the breath toward a longer, more audible exhalation
- create a deeply restful state for body and nervous system

Why the sequence works:
- the opening slows the system before technique begins
- the XLT protocol progressively lengthens and softens the exhale
- meditation carries the nervous system from regulation into inward steadiness
- deep rest lets the effect absorb instead of ending too abruptly
