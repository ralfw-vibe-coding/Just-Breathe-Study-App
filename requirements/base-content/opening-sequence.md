The opening sequence is described in the manual as a deliberate transition into a quiet moment.

The core steps are:

1. Opening invitation
Use the mantra to invite attention inward:
- Close your eyes
- Be still
- Just breathe

2. Stillness
Begin with stillness in body and mind:
- sit
- settle
- stillness
- remove distractions or temptations where possible
- give yourself time to arrive

3. Cultivate awareness
Before moving into technique, become aware of the moment as it is:
- be here completely
- notice what you notice
- feel what you feel

Important to remember:
- The opening is not rushed.
- The invitation may be adapted to a softened gaze when closing the eyes is not appropriate.
- The aim is to move from outer stimulation toward inward attention before any main technique begins.
