title: "Affirmations"
overview: "Supportive statements used to reinforce a chosen inner direction."
---

Affirmations use repeated language to strengthen a chosen state, quality, or direction of mind.

They are useful when the aim is to:
- orient the mind toward support
- reinforce steadiness, confidence, clarity, or compassion
- help an intention become emotionally felt rather than only intellectually understood

Unlike a neutral mantra, an affirmation carries meaning. It is usually chosen because the practice is trying to cultivate something specific.

Why it works:
- repeated language influences attention and expectation
- a clear phrase can interrupt habitual negative loops
- when the words are believable and well chosen, they can gradually reshape inner tone
