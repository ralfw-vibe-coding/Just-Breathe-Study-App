A complete unwinding practice that combines movement, breathwork, meditation, and deep rest.

Structure:
- begin with the opening invitation
- move through `Gentle 10` to prepare the body for stillness
- continue with the `Exhale` practice as the breathwork portion
- enter `Just Breathe Meditation`
- finish with reclined stillness as deep rest
- close with `3 Gratitudes`

Timing:
- 10 minutes `Gentle 10`
- 20 minutes breathwork
- 20 minutes meditation
- 10 minutes deep rest
- total: 60 minutes

Purpose:
- alleviate fatigue and physical tension
- create enough mobility and ease for extended stillness
- support deep release across physical, energetic, mental, emotional, and resting layers
- offer a nourishing full-spectrum unwinding practice

Why the sequence works:
- movement reduces stiffness and makes sitting more available
- breathwork down-regulates the nervous system before meditation
- meditation deepens inwardness after the body and breath have already settled
- deep rest allows the whole practice to land as recovery rather than effort alone

Extended version:
- the three-round version repeats the full 60-minute arc three times
- round 1 is guided for intention
- round 2 is gently guided for attention
- round 3 is self-guided for connection and deeper transcendence
