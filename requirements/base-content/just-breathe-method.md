The manual presents the method through three connected pillars:
- Breathwork
- Meditation
- Deep Rest

Each pillar can be practised on its own, but their combined use is described as exponentially more valuable for conscious unwinding.

Breathwork helps regulate the nervous system through the movement and manipulation of the breathing pattern. Meditation helps quiet the mind, reduce mental load, and return attention to ease and effortlessness. Deep rest creates a dedicated period of conscious unwinding in which the body is allowed to soften into recovery, healing, and rejuvenation.

Important to remember:
- The method is intentionally organised around down-regulation.
- The three pillars are distinct, but designed to work together.
- The aim is practical support for everyday mind and mental health.
