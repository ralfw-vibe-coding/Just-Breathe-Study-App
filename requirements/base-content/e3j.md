An up-regulation protocol built to increase energy, alertness, and upward drive.

The sequence combines three progressively activating techniques:
- `Energising Breath` warms and regulates the flow of air by narrowing the breath across the back of the throat
- `3-Part Interrupted Inhale` expands inhalation in stages and increases respiratory capacity and upward lift
- `Breath of Joy` adds dynamic arm movement, larger inhalations, and a forceful release to create a strong energising effect

Purpose:
- lift low energy
- increase circulation and oxygenation
- sharpen focus
- create a more vivid, alert state

Why the sequence works:
- it starts with contained activation, then builds inhalation capacity, then moves into full-body stimulation
- each step increases respiratory effort without jumping straight to maximum intensity
- the protocol is designed to move the system toward wakefulness rather than rest
