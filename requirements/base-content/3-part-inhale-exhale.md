title: "3-Part Inhale / Exhale"
overview: "A segmented inhale and exhale with short pauses throughout the cycle."
---

How to practice:
Find a comfortable seat or reclined position and take a few clearing breaths.
- Inhale the first third of the breath.
- Pause briefly.
- Inhale the next third.
- Pause briefly.
- Inhale the final third until the lungs are full.
- Pause at the top.
- Exhale one third.
- Pause briefly.
- Exhale the next third.
- Pause briefly.
- Exhale the final third until the lungs are empty.
- Pause again.
- Repeat for a few rounds.

Purpose:
Used to slow the breath and create calm and ease. The emphasis is segmentation, pacing, and greater awareness of the whole breathing cycle.

Why it works:
The manual describes the brief pauses during both inhale and exhale as the element that helps slow the breath down. Breaking the cycle into thirds makes each phase more conscious and reduces the tendency to rush through the breath.
