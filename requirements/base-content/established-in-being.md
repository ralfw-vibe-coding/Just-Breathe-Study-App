title: "Established in Being"
overview: "A non-doing approach that lets awareness settle beyond effort."
---

Established in Being is the contemplative depth of meditation concerned with being rather than doing.

What defines it:
- non-doing contemplative practice
- going deeply inward
- non-striving transcendence
- less disturbance from the thinking mind or ego
- awareness that rests rather than performs

These practices fit when the method asks for quiet presence, surrender, or deep inward settling rather than active concentration or narrative.

- **Being Technique**: a direct method for resting in presence.
- **Just Breathe Meditation**: simple breath-based settling used as a doorway into stillness.
- **Being Mantra Meditation**: mantra used in a way that supports inward settling rather than concentration.
- **Mantra Practice**: mantra work that supports this deeper state of presence.
- **Stillness / Non-doing Practice**: practices centered on silence, openness, and letting go of effort.

Purpose:
- de-stimulate the mind
- reduce attachment triggers
- cultivate awareness without strain
- support an effortless inward state
