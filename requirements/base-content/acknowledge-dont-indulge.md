---
id: acknowledge-dont-indulge
title: "Acknowledge, don’t indulge"
overview: "Notice what is happening without feeding it further."
---

The principle is simple: recognise what is arising, but do not keep giving it extra fuel.

To acknowledge means:
- notice the thought, feeling, sensation, or urge
- allow it to be real
- avoid denial or suppression

Not to indulge means:
- do not keep building the story around it
- do not let one thought become a long chain of thoughts
- do not assume every inner event deserves more attention

This is especially important in meditation because wandering thoughts are normal. The work is not to become thoughtless, but to recognise distraction and return to the technique.
