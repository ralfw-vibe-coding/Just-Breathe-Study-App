An up-regulation protocol that uses one technique in three speeds to build heat and activation progressively.

The protocol repeats `Cleansing & Clearing Breath` as:
- slow variation
- medium variation
- fast variation

Purpose:
- increase internal heat
- stimulate circulation
- raise metabolic effort
- build energising intensity in a graded way

Why the sequence works:
- the active abdominal exhale strengthens effort and creates heat
- the passive inhale allows the rhythm to continue without overcomplicating the technique
- moving from slow to medium to fast lets activation rise in stages instead of all at once

Important to remember:
- Speed should increase without losing clean technique.
- The aim is progressive stimulation, not rushed effort.
