A combined practice that pairs movement preparation with the signature down-regulating breath sequence.

Structure:
- start with `Gentle 10` to create space, mobility, and readiness for stillness
- continue directly into `Exhale`

Purpose:
- reduce physical tension before quieter practice
- make the body more available for sitting, breathing, and unwinding
- deepen the effect of `Exhale` by removing some of the bodily resistance first

Why the sequence works:
- movement prepares the physical body before breathwork asks it to settle
- the shift from mobility into extended exhale work creates a clear downward arc
- the combination is strong when someone needs both somatic preparation and nervous-system regulation

Important to remember:
- this pairing is the basis of `Full Unwinding`
- the movement is not separate from the unwinding effect; it helps make that effect possible
