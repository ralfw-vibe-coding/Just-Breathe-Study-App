title: "Inspired Pause"
overview: "A breath practice that highlights the pause after inhalation."
---

How to practice:
Find a comfortable seat and begin with a conscious breath.
- Inhale slowly to a comfortable and manageable level.
- Pause without force and without distinct muscular gripping.
- Hold for about 2 seconds or a similar slow count.
- Exhale gently.
- Increase or decrease the pause as preferred.

Purpose:
Used to create non-physical ease, slow the heart rate, and expand lung capacity. The emphasis is the intentional pause rather than only the movement of air.

Why it works:
The manual stresses that value in breathing is not only in inhaling and exhaling but also in the moments when we are not breathing in or out. The significant pause shifts attention into that gap and is presented as a way to slow the heart rate and increase spaciousness in the breath.
