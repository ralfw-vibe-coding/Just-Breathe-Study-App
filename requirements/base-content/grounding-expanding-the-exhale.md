This ABC123 sequence uses exhale-focused techniques to ground the system and guide it toward steadiness.

Structure:
- begin with the Just Breathe invitation, stillness, awareness, observation of the breath, and a conscious breath
- `1. Pinhole Breath`
- `2. Interrupted Exhale` with count variations: `4:4`, `4:6`, `4:8`
- `3. Alternate Nostril Breath x 8`
- close with conscious breath, renewed awareness, stillness, and `3 Gratitudes`

Purpose:
- lengthen and refine the exhale
- reduce activation and scattered breathing
- create a more grounded internal state

Why the sequence works:
- pinhole breath immediately slows the release
- interrupted exhale trains control across progressively longer ratios
- alternate nostril breathing helps the grounded effect become smoother and more balanced
