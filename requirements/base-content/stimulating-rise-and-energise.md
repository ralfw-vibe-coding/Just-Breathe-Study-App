This ABC123 sequence is built to raise activation and help the system feel more energised and available.

Structure:
- begin with the Just Breathe invitation, stillness, awareness, observation of the breath, and a conscious breath
- `1. Energising Breath x 12`
- `2. Cleansing & Clearing Breath` at moderate speed with touch variations: using hands to pump, light touch, no hands
- `3. Interrupted Inhale` with count variations: `4:4`, `6:4`, `8:4`
- close with conscious breath, renewed awareness, stillness, and `3 Gratitudes`

Purpose:
- raise energy
- stimulate circulation
- build momentum and inner brightness
- move out of flatness or sluggishness

Why the sequence works:
- energising breath starts the upward shift through heat and respiratory activation
- cleansing and clearing adds stronger abdominal work and stimulation
- interrupted inhale consolidates the uplift by increasing inhale length and drive
