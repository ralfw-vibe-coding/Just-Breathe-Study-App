title: "Quality / Quantity / Capacity Axis"
overview: "A simple way to compare what changes, how much changes, and what can be sustained."
---

Use this axis to separate three different questions.

- **Quality** asks: what is the character of the breath?
  - smooth or uneven
  - calm or agitated
  - natural or forced
  - soft or strained

- **Quantity** asks: how much breath or technique is being used?
  - length of inhale or exhale
  - number of rounds
  - breathing rate
  - ratio or count

- **Capacity** asks: how much can the system hold well?
  - Can the pattern be sustained?
  - Does the person stay steady?
  - Is there enough tolerance for the technique?
  - Does the body remain workable rather than strained?

These distinctions stop everything from being lumped together. A breath may increase in quantity without improving quality. A pattern may look good in theory but exceed capacity in practice. This axis helps breathwork stay responsive, safe, and honest.
