This ABC123 sequence is built for a softer descent into regulation and rest.

Structure:
- begin with the Just Breathe invitation, stillness, awareness, observation of the breath, and a conscious breath
- `1. Alternate Nostril Breath` support variations: alternate nostril, opposite alternate nostril exhale, circular alternate nostril
- `2. Extended Exhale`
- `3. 3-Part Breath Inhale-Exhale` with seat variations: seated, reclined, rested
- close with conscious breath, renewed awareness, stillness, and `3 Gratitudes`

Purpose:
- reduce intensity without becoming vague
- soften the breathing pattern and the overall inner tone
- prepare the body and mind for deeper unwinding

Why the sequence works:
- the nostril practices slow and organise attention first
- extended exhale deepens down-regulation
- the three-part breath variations let the practitioner settle more fully according to position and support
