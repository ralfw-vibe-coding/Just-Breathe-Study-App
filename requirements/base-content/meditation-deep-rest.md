title: "Meditation + Deep Rest"
overview: "A quiet pairing that moves from inward attention into full settling."
---

This pairing begins with meditation and resolves in deep rest.

Purpose:
- quiet the mind without needing a long breathwork phase first
- support inwardness, contemplation, and emotional settling
- allow meditation to land more deeply through a final period of non-doing and stillness

Why the sequence works:
- meditation gathers and steadies the mind
- deep rest gives the body permission to fully receive what the meditation opened
- the transition from attention into conscious unwinding can be especially supportive when the aim is softness rather than intensive regulation

This combination is well suited to days when a quieter entry is enough and the main need is to move from mental activity toward surrender and rest.
