title: "The Big Six"
overview: "A six-part model of common everyday stress triggers."
---

The Big Six names the everyday triggers that often create pressure, overload, and nervous-system reactivity.

The six areas are:
- Situational Occurrences
- Time
- Responsibilities
- Emotions
- Societal Expectation
- Sleep

Important to remember:
- The model is practical rather than abstract.
- Its purpose is to make stress easier to recognise in daily life.
