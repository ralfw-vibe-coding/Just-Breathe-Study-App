This ABC123 sequence is designed to awaken the breath and create a more empowered, alert state.

Structure:
- begin with the Just Breathe invitation, stillness, awareness, observation of the breath, and a conscious breath
- `1. Even Breath` with gaze variations: narrow gaze, tip of the nose, inward gaze
- `2. Bellows Breath 3 x 30`
- `3. Rise & Fall Inhale` with count variations: `4:1:4:1`, `6:1:4:1`, `8:2:4:1`, then back down
- close with conscious breath, renewed awareness, stillness, and `3 Gratitudes`

Purpose:
- sharpen focus
- awaken respiratory power
- support confidence, clarity, and directed activation

Why the sequence works:
- even breath with gaze control gathers and directs attention first
- bellows breath adds strong energetic activation
- rise-and-fall inhale uses counted structure to turn raw activation into more deliberate respiratory control
