A restorative NSRT protocol for reducing stimulus and supporting deep relaxation.

The sequence combines:
- `Coherent Breathing`
- `Alternate Nostril Breathing`
- `Pinhole Breath`

Purpose:
- quiet the system
- lower external and internal stimulation
- support relaxation over a sustained period
- help the body remain with rest instead of only touching it briefly

Why the sequence works:
- coherent breathing slows the respiratory rhythm and supports heart rate variability
- alternate nostril breathing balances the breathing pattern and supports concentration and calm
- pinhole breath extends the exhale and deepens down-regulation

The overall effect is less sensory drive, less urgency, and a more stable restorative state.
