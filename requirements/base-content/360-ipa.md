360 IPA is a balanced protocol used to return the breath toward homeostasis or to maintain an already steady state.

### How the techniques work together
- **360 Breathing** builds full torso breath capacity. It encourages even expansion through the rib cage and supports a fuller, more distributed breath.
- **Inspired Pause** slows the breath by adding a deliberate pause at the top of the inhale. This can settle the system, ease the heart rate, and create space before the exhale.
- **Alternate Nostril Breathing** adds rhythm and attention to the breath. It helps steady the breathing pattern and supports calm, balance, and overall equilibrium.

Used together, these techniques create a balanced practice that restores rather than drives the breath. The aim is a calm, even rhythm you can return to whenever you need support.

Suggested flow:
1. build full torso support with 360 Breathing
2. introduce spaciousness with Inspired Pause
3. refine balance and concentration with Alternate Nostril Breathing

The overall effect is regulation without urgency.
