title: "Breathwork + Meditation + Deep Rest"
overview: "A three-part arc from regulation into inwardness and recovery."
---

This combination follows the three pillars of the wider method in their clearest order:
- breathwork first
- meditation second
- deep rest last

Purpose:
- regulate the nervous system through the breath
- quiet and focus the mind through meditation
- let the effect absorb through stillness and recovery

Why the sequence works:
- breathwork gives the body a direct physiological entry point
- meditation is often easier once the breath has already shifted the state
- deep rest completes the arc by allowing recovery, healing, and integration instead of stopping at technique

This is one of the most complete combinations because it moves from active regulation into inward awareness and finally into conscious unwinding.
