title: "Energising Breath"
overview: "A throat-regulated breath that warms, energises, and steadies the mind."
---

How to practice:
Begin by learning the throat action.
- Exhale as though fogging a mirror and notice the gentle washing sound at the back of the throat.
- Inhale with the same sound.
- Practise this for a few rounds.
- Keep the same throat activation but close the lips and exhale through the nose.
- Practise for a few more rounds.
- Then keep the mouth closed and breathe in and out through the nose with the same sound on both inhale and exhale.
- Continue for about one minute and then release.

Purpose:
Used to regulate the breath, warm and energise the body, and calm the mind. The emphasis is controlled throat activation together with a steady sounding breath.

Why it works:
The manual says the practice constricts the epiglottis so the breath washes across the back of the throat. That mechanical narrowing regulates airflow while also creating warmth and a more controlled respiratory rhythm.
