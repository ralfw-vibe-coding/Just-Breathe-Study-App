**How to practice**
1. Stand with the feet about shoulder-width apart and the knees slightly bent.
2. Inhale the first third of the breath through the nose while swinging the arms forward and up.
3. Inhale the second third through the nose while opening the arms out to the sides.
4. Inhale to full capacity through the nose while bringing the arms overhead again.
5. Exhale strongly through the mouth with a `ha` sound as the knees bend and the body folds forward.
6. Repeat for a few rounds, then pause and notice the effect.

**Purpose**
- increase energy
- uplift mood and sensation
- enhance circulation
- sharpen focus and readiness

**Why it works**
- staged inhalations increase respiratory volume and wakefulness
- the arm movements recruit the whole body and amplify the energising effect
- the forceful release clears tension and creates a vivid sensory shift toward activation
