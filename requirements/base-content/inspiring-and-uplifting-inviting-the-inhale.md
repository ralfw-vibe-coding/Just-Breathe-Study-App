This ABC123 sequence is designed to inspire, uplift, and increase activation through inhale-led techniques.

Structure:
- begin with the Just Breathe invitation, stillness, awareness, observation of the breath, and a conscious breath
- `1. Bellows Breath` with round variations: `1 x 30`, `3 x 30`, `5 x 30`
- `2. Interrupted Inhale x 8`
- `3. Opposite Alternate Nostril Inhale x 8`
- close with conscious breath, renewed awareness, stillness, and `3 Gratitudes`

Purpose:
- lift low energy
- strengthen inspiratory effort
- create an alert, positive upward shift

Why the sequence works:
- bellows breath creates immediate activation
- interrupted inhale develops inhalation length and capacity
- opposite alternate nostril inhale keeps the activation organised and mentally focused
