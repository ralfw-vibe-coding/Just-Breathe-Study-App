---

How to practice:
Find a comfortable seat and bring awareness to the lower belly.
- Take a long inhale through the nose.
- Contract the abdominal muscles in quick but regulated succession to force out short exhalations.
- Let the inhalation happen passively in response to the active exhalation.
- Continue for about one minute.
- Finish with a deep inhale through the nostrils and a slow exhale through the mouth.

Purpose:
Used to create heat in the body, strengthen the abdomen, aid digestion, and clear the nadis. The emphasis is active abdominal exhalation with passive inhalation.

Why it works:
The manual explicitly identifies the active exhale and passive inhale as the defining mechanism. Repeated abdominal contractions create the pumping action that builds heat, strengthens the abdominal area, and gives the technique its clearing quality.
