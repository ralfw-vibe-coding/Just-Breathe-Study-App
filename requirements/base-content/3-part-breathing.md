title: "3-Part Breathing"
overview: "Deep belly-and-chest breathing for oxygen intake and full-body calm."
---

How to practice:
Find a comfortable seat and notice the current pace of the breath.
- Take a conscious breath to begin.
- Place one hand on the belly and one hand on the chest.
- Breathe slowly and fully into both belly and chest equally.
- Feel both hands rise and expand.
- Pause at the top for about 1 second.
- Exhale slowly and let belly and chest empty.
- Repeat for 10 rounds or about 60 seconds.

Purpose:
Used to increase oxygen intake and create a full-body sense of calmness. The emphasis is internal movement of the breath together with a warming quality through the body.

Why it works:
The manual describes this as a deep-breathing technique that moves through both belly and chest. That combined expansion increases the felt movement of breath through the torso and is presented as useful both for calming the nerves and awakening the senses.
